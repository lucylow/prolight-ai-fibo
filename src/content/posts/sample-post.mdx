# Introducing ProLight AI

ProLight AI is a cutting-edge platform that brings **precision lighting control** to photographers and e-commerce professionals. Our platform combines advanced AI models with the FIBO (Fiber-Optic Based Illumination) standard to deliver reproducible, studio-quality lighting setups.

## What Makes ProLight AI Different?

### 1. **AI-Powered Lighting Analysis**

Our platform analyzes your images and suggests optimal lighting configurations based on:

- Subject composition
- Desired mood and style
- Camera settings
- Environmental factors

### 2. **FIBO Integration**

ProLight AI fully supports the FIBO JSON format, ensuring compatibility with industry-standard lighting equipment and workflows.

### 3. **Real-Time Preview**

See your lighting changes in real-time with our Three.js-powered 3D preview. Adjust lights, camera angles, and see the results instantly.

## Getting Started

1. **Upload Your Image**: Start with a product photo or portrait
2. **Analyze Lighting**: Let our AI suggest improvements
3. **Fine-Tune**: Adjust lighting parameters to match your vision
4. **Export**: Generate FIBO JSON or render final images

## Use Cases

- **E-commerce Product Photography**: Consistent, professional product shots
- **Portrait Photography**: Studio-quality lighting for portraits
- **Fashion Photography**: Dynamic lighting setups for fashion shoots
- **Architectural Photography**: Precise lighting for architectural subjects
